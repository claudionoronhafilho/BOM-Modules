BoM Product Cost

Calculate Total Bill of Material Cost of Product and Show BoM Cost in BoM Form and Product Form.

This module allow to calculate total bill of material cost of product and show bom cost in bom form view, product template and product variant form view. Cost calculate based on bom product line and quantity. Calculate BoM cost price of Manufacturing Product or BoM product. Users can see BoM Product Cost on Product Template. Users can see BoM Product Cost on Product Variant.

Bills of Materials List View

Go to Manufacturing -> Master Data -> Bills of Materials then we can see Total BoM Cost.

Bills of Materials Form View

Cost and Total Cost fields added for BoM Product Line

Total BoM Cost field for BoM Form

Total BoM Cost on Product Template

Total BoM Cost on Product Variant

Note: This module is tested and working in Odoo Community Edition but not tested in Enterprise Edition.
